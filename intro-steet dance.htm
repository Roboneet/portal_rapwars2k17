STREET DANCE
Street Dance is a freestyle group dance event aimed at giving rise to the hip-hop, b-boying, popping and locking funk dance culture.

RULES
  • Minimum 5 and maximum 14 dancers per team.
  • One entry per college
  • The duration of the performance should be 5-10 min.
  • Teams must bring a pen-drive containing their soundtrack. The soundtrack should be in .mp3 format.
  • The dance floor area must be in the same condition after the performances as before.
  • No props will be provided. Teams may use props if they want, for which prior permission from the Coordinator of Department of Theatre is necessary. Negative points for usage of props without permission.
  • Participants should show the freestyle form of street dance. Power movements, acrobatics and such are admissible, but they should not form a major part of the performance.
  • Individuality and originality of the dance is encouraged as well as interaction and contact with the other dancers.
  • Dancers are not restricted from performing any move but they must be aware that it is their own responsibility to ensure that they do not perform dangerous moves that could injure themselves or others. Coordinators have the right to bring in safety restrictions.
  • Lighting of candles, matches or cigarettes on stage is not allowed and will result in disqualification.
  • In case of situations which have been not described in rules, decision taken by judges will be final.
